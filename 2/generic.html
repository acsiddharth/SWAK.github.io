Happy Anniversary and Valentine's Day

14.02.2018

In all the world, there is no heart for me like yours. In all the world, there is no love for you like mine.

On our anniversary, I want you to know how much I’ve enjoyed annoying you all this time and how excited I am to keep doing it in the future

The best love is the kind that awakens the soul; that makes us reach for more, that plants the fire in our hearts and brings peace to our minds. That’s what I hope to give you forever

Forever is a measure of time used by people who share an ordinary love. Our extraordinary love is immeasurable … for us, forever just won’t do.

Grow old with me. The best is yet to be!

One year down and forever to go. Happy anniversary.

As I wish you on our anniversary, I find it hard to explain how my life has changed for the better… ever since the day I fell in love with you

I’ve forgotten all about the day we met for the first time, because life has been a sweet blur since the moment you became mine.

You are my today and all of my tomorrows

Meeting you was fate, becoming your friend was a choice, but falling in love with you I had no control over.

Like the smiles in our selfies, you light up my life’s memories… I love you. Happy anniversary.

Yet another year of our relationship, as we break into – I hope that I get to celebrate this again and again with you.

There is a special place in my heart for you and it is called PERMANENT.

I haven’t seen the future but if our past is anything to go by, it is going to be fantastic and romantic, amazing and exciting, heady and steady.

As quickly as it came along, this anniversary will go. But my love for you, will never stop to grow.

Our relationship and my love for you isn’t bound by dates, days or time. But I still remember this day because it is the day when your heart became mine.
